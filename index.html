The Problem

Millions of women know how to create.
Few have the tools to grow.

Talented makers across India are held back not by skill, but by four invisible barriers to digital business.

No Digital Catalog

Products are sold off WhatsApp screenshots — no organized listings, no consistent presentation.

Pricing Uncertainty

Without cost & market data, women either undercharge for their labour or lose customers by overpricing.

Marketing Challenges

No time or tools to design posters, write captions, or run promotions that actually reach buyers.

Low Digital Literacy

Complex apps in English assume a comfort with screens and text that many entrepreneurs haven't had the chance to build.
